Curriculum vitae

My name is Bill Nighy. If you are looking for a science guy, you got the wrong Bill, I am an actor! My last name is also pronounced slightly different from the science guy's, but I won't go into that here. I was born on December 12th 1949 in Caterham, Surrey, to Catherine Josephine Nighy and Alfred Martin Nighy, and I currently reside in Pimlico, London.

I have acting experience in many forms, from stage to cinema and TV as well as radio and voice acting for video games, such as the Destiny series. Apart from acting, I am also known for my support of total gender equality. The highlighting of gender inequality in my industry has influenced my choice of film roles. My role in Pride is one of my most cherished.

Suits are the clothes of my choice, preferrably navy colored. I was actually listed as one of the 50 best-dressed over-50 by The Guardian in March 2013, and one of GQ's 50 best-dressed British men in 2015.
